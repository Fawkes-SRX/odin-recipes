How To: Prepare and cook a batch of cookies!

This is a recipe for how to prepare and cook a batch of cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Mix the wet ingredients
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Dough the cookies
  7. Cook the cookies
  8. Enjoy your cookies
