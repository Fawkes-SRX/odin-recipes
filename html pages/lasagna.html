How To: Prepare and cook a lasagna!

This is a recipe for how to prepare and cook a lasagna

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the flour
  2. Prepare the tomato sauce
  3. Prepare the pepperoni
  4. Prepare the olives
  5. Prepare the mushrooms
  6. Prepare the onions
  7. Prepare the lasagna noodles
  8. Prepare the cheese
  9. Prepare the lasagna
  10. Cook the lasagna
  11. Enjoy your lasagna
